					<h1>The Don Vicente Gullas Learning Resource Center</h1>
				</div>
			</div> <!-- .et_pb_text --><div class="et_pb_module et_pb_text et_pb_text_26 et_pb_bg_layout_light  et_pb_text_align_justified">
				
				
				<div class="et_pb_text_inner">
					<p style="text-align: left;">The Don Vicente Learning Resource Center (DVLRC), is a state of the art library of the University of the Visayas. It occupies the 3rd and 4th floors of the Inday Pining Building which is situated in the heart of the University’s Main Campus. It is fully air-conditioned, equipped with CCTV cameras installed in different strategic places, and comfortable furniture to make study and research more conducive. It has also acquired the latest RFID Security gate (the first in Cebu) to ensure the safety of its resources. Discussion rooms for small and big groups are also available for the students to utilize. A Visualization Room which can accommodate 60 persons is also afforded for the faculty and students to use for their class presentations, product demonstrations, and the like. A forty seater (40) internet laboratory is also available for all students for their study and research needs.</p>
<p style="text-align: left;">The DVLRC supports both the students and faculty in their academic and research needs through the provision of varied updated information sources, both printed and online. It is also manned by qualified and efficient librarians who are always available to serve the whole academe.</p>
